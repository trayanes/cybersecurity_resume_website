About me

Hello! I'm Trayan Chernev, a ski coach and instructor who's explored the slopes of Switzerland and New Zealand. Beyond the thrill of the mountains, my journey has been one of discovery, resilience, and a profound passion for cybersecurity. Since childhood, computers have captivated me, leading me to dive deeper into this fascinating field. My career path, starting in aviation and transitioning into cybersecurity, has shaped me into a versatile professional. Now, I'm committed to becoming a cybersecurity analyst, blending my technological enthusiasm with my coaching instincts. Excited about the future, I look forward to contributing to the cybersecurity community and securing our digital landscape together. My experiences have not only equipped me with technical skills but also instilled in me a deep sense of responsibility towards safeguarding digital spaces. As I navigate this exciting transition, I'm eager to bring my unique perspective to the table, combining my analytical prowess with my empathetic nature honed through years of coaching. Together, let's forge a path towards a more secure digital future.

Experience

I have hands-on experience with various operating systems, including Linux and Windows, and their terminals. This has given me a strong foundation in managing and troubleshooting different computing environments. My cybersecurity journey has equipped me with fundamental networking knowledge, enabling me to understand and secure system communications effectively. I've worked extensively with Kali Linux for security testing and am familiar with the OWASP Top 10, essential for identifying and mitigating common web vulnerabilities. In a Security Operations Center (SOC) setting, I've used tools like Splunk for security information and event management (SIEM), enhancing my skills in detecting and responding to security incidents. Additionally, I have experience with Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS), further strengthening my ability to protect digital environments. On the cloud front, I possess basic knowledge of Microsoft Azure, which helps me understand and leverage cloud services efficiently. Through these experiences, I've developed a proactive approach to cybersecurity, blending technical skills with a commitment to safeguarding our digital spaces. Let's work together towards a more secure digital future!
